
# Colors and Theme Used in [app/page.tsx](cci:7://file:///c:/Users/shubh/Desktop/anonx-mvp/app/page.tsx:0:0-0:0)

## Main Colors Used

- **Background:**  
  - `bg-black` (Tailwind) → #000000

- **Gradients and Highlights:**  
  - Radial gradient: `rgba(29, 78, 216, 0.15)` (blue-700, #1d4ed8 with 15% opacity)
  - Grid lines: `#4f4f4f12` (gray with 7% opacity)
  - Gradient text and backgrounds:  
    - `linear-gradient(90deg, #60a5fa, #a78bfa, #ec4899)`  
      - #60a5fa (blue-400)  
      - #a78bfa (purple-400)  
      - #ec4899 (pink-500)
    - `bg-gradient-to-r from-blue-600 via-purple-600 to-pink-600`
      - from: #2563eb (blue-600)
      - via: #9333ea (purple-600)
      - to:   #ec4899 (pink-500)

- **Orbs:**  
  - `bg-blue-500` → #3b82f6  
  - `bg-purple-500` → #a78bfa  
  - `bg-pink-500` → #ec4899

- **Text:**  
  - `text-gray-400` → #9ca3af  
  - `text-white` → #ffffff

- **Borders and Accents:**  
  - `border-white/10` → #ffffff with 10% opacity  
  - `border-blue-500/20` → #3b82f6 with 20% opacity  
  - `border-purple-500/20` → #a78bfa with 20% opacity  
  - `hover:border-blue-500` → #3b82f6

- **Shadows:**  
  - `hover:shadow-[0_0_20px_rgba(59,130,246,0.3)]`  
    - rgba(59,130,246,0.3) → #3b82f6, 30% opacity

## Theme Summary

- **Dark theme:** Dominant black background with soft, colorful gradients and accent orbs.
- **Primary accent colors:** Blue, purple, pink (in gradients and orbs).
- **Text:** Mostly gray-400 for secondary, white for primary.
- **Borders:** Subtle, using blue/purple with low opacity.
- **Modern, vibrant, and minimal.**

# Recommended Color Palette for Your Whole Website

- **Black:** #000000 (background)
- **Blue:** #3b82f6 (primary accent), #2563eb (gradient), #60a5fa (gradient)
- **Purple:** #a78bfa (accent), #9333ea (gradient)
- **Pink:** #ec4899 (accent)
- **Gray:** #4f4f4f (grid), #9ca3af (text)
- **White:** #ffffff (text, borders at low opacity)

**Use gradients:**  
`linear-gradient(90deg, #60a5fa, #a78bfa, #ec4899)`  
or  
`bg-gradient-to-r from-blue-600 via-purple-600 to-pink-600`
